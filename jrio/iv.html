John Robison

John Robison Hoopes is an explorer, researcher and entrepreneur based in Colorado. His purpose is foggy, but coming into focus - pointing towards the confluence of design, data and innovation to create products for positive impact. John was once a materialist but somehow opened his eyes to the subtle simplicity of quality, in Bosnia in 2012. Since then the pursuit has only been accelerating, like most things these days.

John currently works in Colorado, but hopes to move to London this autumn to enter a masters program. He may be available for contract work. iv@johnrobison.io
